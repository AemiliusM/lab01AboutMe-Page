My Name is Sampson

Let me tell you about myself!
I love to start my day by jumping on my sleeping parents heads.
After they are awake, I clean myself as I prepare for battle with my arch-nemesis.
He is my brother, Hippo. I hiss and swat at him all day no matter if he is sleeping or eating.
Once I have won the battle for the day, I clean myself again. After cleaning I will let out my battle cry "LABABALABA" and expel the fur of my enemy on the floor.
Though I clean myself daily, my parents insist upon a monthly washing. No matter how I cry and fight, they ignore my pain to this torture.
They think catnip will ease my mind, but they have to sleep sometime.
